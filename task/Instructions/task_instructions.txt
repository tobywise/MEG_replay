Now that you've become familiar with the pictures, we're going to begin the task.

*You will move through the pictures in this task by pressing the number keys, like in the last phase. However, here you'll see the start image on the screen, and then it is up to you to move to another end image using what you learned about the pictures in the training.

*You will enter three movements by pressing the number keys corresponding to the images you want to choose on the grid, then you will be shown which pictures you have moved through. When the third image is displayed on the screen, you may see a a picture of some coins. 

*Remember, your goal is to find the picture that is mostl likely to give you money.

*If you don't have any questions, press 1 to begin.
